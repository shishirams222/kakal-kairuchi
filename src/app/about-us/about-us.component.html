  • The Inner Spark 1987
    Initial Days...
    Mr. Sathish Kakal, born on 19th November, 1971 as 4th son of Smt. Jayalaxmi and Shri. Kakal Lakshminarayana Rao. An Electrical Engineer, has spent his childhood assisting his parents in the family's catering business along with his schooling till he relocated to Bengaluru for his higher studies. He has an inborn talent of Business Management
