Journey Begins

1970...

A legacy started as a Hostel Mess in 1970 serving authentic Carnatic Feast to the locals and students at Heggodu (Shivamogga Dist) by beloved parents Smt. Jayalaxmi and Shri. Kakal Lakshminarayana Rao

The Inner Spark

1987

Mr. Sathish Kakal, born on 19th November, 1971 as 4th son of Smt. Jayalaxmi and Shri. Kakal Lakshminarayana Rao. An Electrical Engineer, has spent his childhood assisting his parents in the family's catering business along with his schooling till he relocated to Bengaluru for his higher studies. He has an inborn talent of Business Management

Midas Touch

1997

Life and dream intensified with life partner Mrs. Chaya Kakal, who is specialist in cooking with gifted knowledge about natural nutritional values of various plants and carries a Midas Touch where food is concerned

Utsav Catering Co.

2001 - 2010

The zeal of Mr. Sathish Kakal later transformed this legacy as Utsav Catering Company in 2001 in Bengaluru

Kakal Kai Ruchi!

13 May, 2010

Transformation was further flavoured by Mrs. Chaya Kakal to evolve 'Taste Of Hand' transform into brand "KAKAL KAI RUCHI". A multi-cuisine restaurant to satisfy all cultures.
