Humble beginnings...

1970...

A legacy started as a Hostel Mess in 1970 serving authentic Carnatic Feast to the locals and students at Heggodu (Shivamogga Dist) by Smt. Jayalaxmi and Shri. Kakal Lakshminarayana Rao

The Childhood Passion

1987

Mr. Sathish Kakal, academically an electrical engineer, developed a serious passion for the food business while assisting his parents in the family business of catering in his early childhood.

Passion meets destiny

1997

Passion intensified when he married Mrs. Chaya Kakal, who had a natural flair for cooking with an inate understanding and knowledge of nutritional cooking.

Passion in action

2001 - 2010

The passion of Mr. Sathish Kakal later transformed the early legacy of catering as Utsav Catering Company an institutional catering service provider in Bengaluru.

Kai Ruchi was born

13 May, 2010

From the initial institutional catering service provider, the Kakal's ventured into brand "KAI RUCHI", a multi-cuisine restaurant to provide authentic, delicious and hygenic food.

Nothing succeeds like success

2012 - 2017

Twelwe years since its inception, the brand 'Kai Ruchi' has is outlets in 12 prominent locations in the city of Bengaluru.
